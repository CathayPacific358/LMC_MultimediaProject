Morning 7 a.m.

Wakeup, get up, wash and dress

The identity of myself is a citizen that live in modern society. I need to make myself tidy and clean, wearing in common suits. I am going to meet others today, talking and communicating with them face to face. I need to make sure that there would be no strange smell from my mouth while I am speaking. I also need to wear clothes in student style, which can also be fashion and comfortable. My identity of being a student in the college makes me act in this way.

Morning 7:30 a.m.

Have breakfast

My identity in having breakfast is influenced by my preference on food. I would prefer eastern food for breakfast especially different sorts of dimsums from Guangdong. I would also like to have a cup of soymilk for drink rather than having coffee or milk. However, I cannot eat most of the spicy food as Zhuhai is a costal city that would be too humid to take too much "hot food". Thus, I have long been eating cantonese cuisines that having light salt and pepper.

Morning 8 a.m.

Have classes

The identity of myself is a Chinese student in the classroom. I need to keep quiet when the teacher is speaking in the front of the classroom because the traditional chinese education told me to do so, although teachers encourage us to speak more. However, anyway, It is better for me to listen to the class. It is not polite to chat with others both by whisper and online or asking question after raising hand. While discussing with the classmates, it is polite to listen to others carefully.

Noon 12 p.m.

Stay with girlfriend

The identity of this time is a boyfriend. We share interesting things surround us to each other, have lunch together; we may have hugs, flirting each other; we encourage each other when we encounters some troubles; we may imagine how our future would be or planning to have a trip when the semester ends.

Afternoon 4 p.m.

Have a football match

The identity of myself is a team member in a sport game. I need to work together with my team members. We have the same goals at that time. The goal leads us to cooperate with each other, fighting for the best results.

Evening 8 p.m.

Have a group discussion

As the leader in this project, I am also the leader who is holding the meeting right now. I am listening to their ideas and advices that they have given to us. I try to figure out the reasonable part and best solution from the information that they give. I try to allocate the tasks into several parts that everyone has his/her resonable and balanced workload.

Evening 10 p.m.

Have a chat with parents

After getting back to the dormitory, I may call my parents as the identity of a son. I share something happen during the day, complain some trouble I encounter, letting them know I am fine, concerning about their health and ask them to take care of themselves.

Evening 11 p.m.

Working quitely

After finishing the chat, my identity will change back to roomate. It is almost midnight and my roomates are already fallen asleep. Thereby, I need to make my action lighter, finish my own stuff without bothering them.
